Hogwarts Castle

Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry is a renowned magical institution where young witches and wizards receive formal magical education. The school is located in the Scottish Highlands and is widely regarded as one of the finest magical schools in the wizarding world.

The Founders

Hogwarts was founded over a thousand years ago by four witches and wizards, each with their own values and vision:

  • Godric Gryffindor valued bravery and courage.
  • Salazar Slytherin valued ambition and resourcefulness.
  • Rowena Ravenclaw valued intelligence and wisdom.
  • Helga Hufflepuff valued loyalty and hard work.

Together, they created Hogwarts as a safe place for students to learn and grow, a legacy that continues to shape the school today.

Headmaster

The Headmaster of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry is responsible for overseeing the school, maintaining its traditions, and ensuring the safety and education of all students.

The current Headmaster is Albus Dumbledore, widely respected for his intelligence, fairness, and commitment to protecting students. Under his leadership, Hogwarts remains a place of learning, courage, and moral guidance even during difficult times.

The Headmaster works closely with professors and staff to uphold the values of the school and guide students in both academic and personal development.
